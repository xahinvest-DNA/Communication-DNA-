# RESEARCH QUESTIONS

## Core research questions
1. What exact personal scenarios create the strongest pull to use this product?
2. Which user questions are frequent enough to justify a focused MVP?
3. What outputs actually reduce uncertainty rather than deepen it?
4. Where is the line between helpful interpretation and harmful overreach?
5. What makes users trust the analysis?
6. Which mode has the strongest retention: incoming-message analysis, draft-checking, internal monologue analysis, or pattern review?
7. What form factor creates the lowest friction and highest trust?
8. What privacy promises are necessary for real adoption?
9. Which effects do users report after repeated usage: clarity, dependency, better boundaries, obsession, or something else?

## Later research direction
Compare explicit user demand with hidden repeated jobs. The strongest product may not be the one users describe directly.
