# THEORY LOG

## Purpose
A place for theory fragments, provisional formulations, and evolving observations that are too early for a formal document.

## Initial notes
- The product is likely stronger when framed as navigation in hidden communication structure, not as emotional diagnosis.
- The real object is often not the message itself, but the user’s relationship to ambiguity.
- A powerful mode may be analysis before sending, not only after receiving.
- Internal monologue analysis may become a major differentiator.
- The deepest value may be reducing projection and false hope rather than explaining the other person.

## Logging rule
Write here when the thought matters but is not yet stable enough to become doctrine.
