# PERSONAL MODE MAP

## Definition
Personal mode is any scenario where communication affects the user’s inner state, attachment, self-image, fear, hope, or decision quality.

## Core clusters
- romance and dating;
- ex-relationships;
- friendship;
- family;
- emotionally entangled partnerships;
- authority figures with strong personal charge;
- self-dialogue and unsent drafts.

## Personal mode user states
Typical states include:
- confusion;
- obsession with interpretation;
- hope/fear oscillation;
- resentment;
- guilt;
- urgency to respond;
- freeze and inability to answer;
- desire for contact mixed with fear of exposure.

## Personal mode questions
- What is happening here?
- What does this message likely mean?
- What am I not seeing?
- Am I reading reality or projecting?
- What do I actually want?
- What is the next move?
- Is this contact worth its emotional price?

## Why personal mode matters
This mode has high emotional energy, high recurrence, and potentially strong retention if the product helps without becoming addictive or deceptive.
