# JTBD PERSONAL

## Core explicit jobs
Users may hire the product to:
- understand what the other person means;
- decode ambiguous or contradictory messages;
- decide whether and how to reply;
- prepare for an important conversation;
- de-escalate conflict;
- protect boundaries without destroying contact;
- check whether their interpretation is distorted;
- understand their own desire before acting.

## Hidden jobs
Users may secretly want the product to:
- grant permission to leave or stay;
- reduce uncertainty pain;
- confirm or disconfirm hope;
- expose manipulation;
- stop impulsive texting;
- restore self-respect;
- reveal repetitive relationship scripts;
- convert emotional chaos into a usable decision frame.

## Functional outcome
The user should leave not only with an explanation, but with improved clarity of position and a practical next step.

## Emotional outcome
The user should feel less flooded, less trapped by interpretation loops, and more capable of acting deliberately.

## Caution
A job-to-be-done map must not be confused with a promise of mind-reading. Outputs should preserve uncertainty and evidence boundaries.
